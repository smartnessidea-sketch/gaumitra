About GauMitra

A future-ready platform for Gaushalas, NGOs, Trusts and cow care organizations.

GauMitra helps manage animal records, health care, feeding, milk tracking, rescue operations, donations, sponsorships, staff, volunteers, documents, reports, alerts and multi-branch operations.

The platform is designed to improve transparency, accountability, donor trust and daily operational efficiency for Indian Gaushalas and cow care organizations.
